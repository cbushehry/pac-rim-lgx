About Us

Our Mission

To redefine the freight brokerage industry in the Bay Area by offering unparalleled logistics solutions. Our mission is to simplify shipping for our clients through innovative services, personalized support, and a commitment to reliability. By fostering strong relationships with carriers and leveraging cutting-edge technology, we aim to ensure every shipment is delivered efficiently and on time.

Our Vision

Our vision is to become the leading freight brokerage firm in North America, renowned for transforming the logistics landscape through sustainability, technology, and exceptional service. We envision a world where businesses in Oakland and beyond can effortlessly connect with reliable carriers, making shipping seamless and more accessible than ever before.

Our Values

At the core of Pacific Rim Logistics are our values: Integrity, Innovation, and Inclusivity. We operate with the highest standards of honesty and transparency, invest in the latest logistics technologies to provide superior service, and embrace diversity within our team and partnerships. Our commitment to these values drives every decision we make and the exceptional service we deliver to our clients and partners.

Our Services

Full Truckload Freight

Specializing in full truckload (FTL) services, we connect Oakland-based businesses with a vast network of carriers. Our expertise ensures your goods are transported efficiently across North America, using the most reliable routes for timely delivery.

Less Than Truckload (LTL) Shipping

Our LTL shipping services offer flexible and cost-effective solutions for businesses with smaller freight needs. By pooling shipments headed in the same direction, we optimize your logistics, reducing costs and environmental impact while maintaining speed and reliability.

Expedited Freight Services

For time-sensitive shipments, our expedited freight services provide the fastest transportation options. Leveraging our network of dependable carriers, we ensure your urgent shipments from the Bay Area reach their destination with speed and care, every time.
