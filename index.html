About Us

Our Mission

To redefine the freight brokerage industry in the Bay Area by offering unparalleled logistics solutions. Our mission is to simplify shipping for our clients through innovative services, personalized support, and a commitment to reliability. By fostering strong relationships with carriers and leveraging cutting-edge technology, we aim to ensure every shipment is delivered efficiently and on time.

Premier Trucking Company

KAMAL TRUCKING CORPORATION is a premier trucking company headquartered in Oakland, CA. Our team has been providing outstanding intermodal transport services throughout the western states since 1987. We are currently the house trucker for BNSF Railroad, and we are serving an extensive local commercial and industrial clientele.

Our Values

At the core of Pacific Rim Logistics are our values: Integrity, Innovation, and Inclusivity. We operate with the highest standards of honesty and transparency, invest in the latest logistics technologies to provide superior service, and embrace diversity within our team and partnerships. Our commitment to these values drives every decision we make and the exceptional service we deliver to our clients and partners.
